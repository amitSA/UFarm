About

What is U-Farm?

U-Farm was founded on the idea of helping farmers in India through peer to peer sharing of equipment.

How is this helpful?

In todays digital age, we have seen many successful startups built on the peer to peer renting of resources. These startups have almost every item that could be of use to the common people. But, there is another category in which the most underused resources exist and are still not being rented out. It is the category of farming equipment.

In India, we see lot of farmers who commit sucide due to lack of money to repay loans. The sprayers, planting equipment, harvesting equipment and many others used for farming are generally used only for 10% - 20% of the time. Also, most of these equipment are costly and hence become a burden on the farmers inorder to buy and maintain them. Insted, they could rent these equipment from other farmers who have the equipment and donot have use for it for a few days.

This renting of equipment helps both the lender and the borrower. The lender gets additional money along with the produce, wheras the borrower needs less money than buying and maintaining the machinery. Thereby the farmers could reduce their worries to some extent.
